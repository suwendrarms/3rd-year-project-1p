Drug and Alcohol Policy

Irresponsible behaviour resulting from the misuse of drugs and/or alcohol may damage our reputation and/or business, and as such, is a policy matter. To state our position on alcohol and drugs within the workplace. To minimise the creation of risks caused by or associated with alcohol and drugs at work.

Policy Objectives

  • I. To state our position on alcohol and drugs within the workplace.
  • II. To ensure we comply with appropriate legislation.
  • III. To minimise the creation of risks caused by or associated with alcohol and drugs at work.
  • IV. To have clear rules regarding substance misuse in the workplace.
  • V. To provide employees with training on the adverse health effects of alcohol and drugs.
  • VI. To encourage the early identification of substance misuse
  • VII. To support employees experiencing alcohol and drug problems.
  • VIII. To provide sufficient training and support to line managers to make sure they feel able to support employees experiencing problems.
